FACEBOOK MONETIZATION



If you've ever wondered whether it's really possible

to earn money through Facebook, this guide is for

you.

We'll walk you through the latest updates to

Facebook's monetization policies, give you a

detailed step-by-step process to set up your Page

for monetization, and share the eligibility criteria

you need to meet before you can start earning.




THE REQUIREMENT FOR FACEBOOK MONETIZATION




1. Eligibility for in-stream ads:



Pages need to have at least 1,000 followers,

and meet Facebook's monetization standards.




2. Content guidelines:



Pages need to follow Facebook's content guidelines,

which prohibit Facebook videos or any content that

is violent, sexually explicit, or promotes hate speech

or illegal activities.



3. page regularity:



Users must login into the account atleast 3 times

a day



4. Payment method:



Pages need to have a valid payment method set up

in order to receive payments from Facebook Pay.



5. Country eligibility:


Pages need to be located in a country where

Facebook in-stream ads are available.
